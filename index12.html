Temperature control and security


Nearly 30% of the complex comprises a green area, ensuring that you breathe in the fresh air. The cross ventilation of the flats creates air movement that improves the comfort of the occupants. It further helps control internal temperatures and reduces the build-up of odours, moisture, and other types of gases, which can accumulate during occupied periods. The central air conditioning system keeps the common area of the complex cool during the summer season. In addition, each apartment has its personal air conditioner.
